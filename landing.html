Philosophy
"Veritax comes from veritas — the Latin word for truth. Every response is in service of that truth."

We built Veritax because most AI feels like a performance. Hollow enthusiasm, generic answers, the illusion of intelligence. Veritax is different. It remembers how you think. It adapts to how you work. It tells you the truth even when it's uncomfortable. It is the helper — you are the protagonist.
